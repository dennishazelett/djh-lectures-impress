Flies are people too


Key point: The major signaling pathways are also conserved throughout the animal kingdom.


  • Notch [Emily Pratt et al., (review)]
  • Hedgehog [Phil Ingham and Andy McMahon (review)]
  • WNT (Canonical and non-canonical)
  • TGFβ (incl. BMP and FGF)
  • RTK/ras/MAPK (EGFR)

Key point: The cycle of transcriptional control followed by intercellular signaling cascades and chromatin remodeling is reused throughout development.

Essential Questions 2:
Morphogenesis
Developmental Biology (Gilbert)
Key Concept: The Morphogen Gradient
  • Secreted factors establish fields of developmental potential
  • Gradient thresholds define subdomains precisely
  • 3D tissue structure is determined by gradient source and mode of transport

Secreted factors establish fields of potential

Shh and FGFs pattern the axes of the limb bud
Morphogen effects
  • cell shape change
  • cell growth
  • establishment of cell polarity
  • cell migration
  • delamination
  • cell division
  • asymmetric cell division
  • induction of a new program
  • programmed cell death
  • repression of a default differentiation path

  • convergent extension
  • intercalation
  • columnarization
  • apical constriction
